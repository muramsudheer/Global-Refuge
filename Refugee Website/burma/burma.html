What's Happening?


In the Rakhine state of Mynmar, ethnic conflict between Rakhine Buddhists and Rohingya Muslims has risen to frightening extremes. Discriminatory policies by the Mynmar government have caused hundereds of thousands of Rohingya to flee the country since the late 1970s. Rohingya Muslims and the Rakhine Buddhists have been in a historical argument about the origin of their ethnicities in Mynmar. With the Rohingya being the minority, policies by the majority Buddhist government have inflated to unimaginable extremes such as genocide and warfare.

Demographics

Total number of Rohinya refugees: 420,000

Number of intenally displaced persons: 120,000

Estimated refugees fled since 2012: 168,000
