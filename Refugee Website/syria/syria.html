Syria

What's Happening?


Currently, the situation in Syria is horrendous. Families are fleeing the country to protect themselves from the dangerous chemical attacks conducted by the government regime of Bashar Al Assad. Refugees have been fleeing to Europe, Turkey, Jordan, Lebanon, Greece, and any other countries who will accept them. The destruction caused by the civil war has forced almost 11 million to flee their homes in search for safety.

Demographics

Number of people in need in Syria: 13.5 million

Number of intenally displaced persons: 6.3 million

Number of people in hard to reach area: 4.7 million
