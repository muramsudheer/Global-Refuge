Palestine

What's Happening?


The historical conflict between the people of Israel and Palestine has been prominent since 1940s. However, this brawl has heightened to new extremes. Although both sides have attacked each other, Israeli's have been using inhuman extreme measures and also violating numerous international laws. Isreal has created various illegal settlements in Palestine and has targeted hospitals and schools, killing thousands of innocent Palestinian children in the process. In addition, discriminatory policies enacted by the Israeli government has made life even more miserable for the Palestinians.
